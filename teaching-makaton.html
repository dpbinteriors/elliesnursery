Teaching Makaton

Teaching Makaton

At Ellie’s Nurseries we love teaching our children Makaton from Babies all the way through until preschool. Here are some benefits of Makaton. Incorporating Makaton into early years education and development can offer a strong foundation for children's communication skills, foster an inclusive environment, and support overall developmental milestones. Its benefits extend beyond just the children with additional needs, enriching the learning experience for all children.
  • Age Group: 2 to 3 year old kids
  • Class Size: 20 kids in one class
  • Session Timing: Morning 9:00 am to Afternoon 12:30 pm
What Else We Are Teaching

Skills that Children Will Learn

Enhances Communication

Makaton enables children with speech delays to express themselves effectively, reducing frustration for both them and adults by improving communication and understanding.

Promotes Inclusion

Makaton provides a shared communication platform, helping integrate children of all abilities. Even those without speech difficulties can learn it, fostering inclusivity and understanding among peers.

Boosts Confidence and Social Skills

As children express their needs, feelings, and thoughts, their confidence and self-esteem grow, enhancing communication skills and leading to better social interactions with adults and peers.

Supports Language Development

Makaton is designed to support the development of spoken language. Signs and symbols are used alongside speech, in spoken word order, helping children to understand and learn spoken language as they use and see Makaton being used.

Encourages Learning and Cognitive Development:

The use of signs and symbols can enhance learning by providing visual cues that reinforce understanding of concepts and language. This multisensory approach can aid memory and recall, supporting cognitive development.
