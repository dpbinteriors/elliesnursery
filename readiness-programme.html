School Readiness Programme

School Readiness Programme

School readiness programs play a crucial role in preparing children for the transition to formal schooling, offering a foundation that supports their future learning and development. These programs encompass a range of skills and knowledge that children are encouraged to develop before starting school, including academic, social, emotional, and physical skills. Here are some of the key benefits of school readiness programs in early years child development
  • Age Group: 2 to 3 year old kids
  • Class Size: 20 kids in one class
  • Session Timing: Morning 9:00 am to Afternoon 12:30 pm
What Else We Are Teaching

Skills that Children Will Learn

Enhances Academic Skills

School readiness programs introduce basic academic concepts like letters, numbers, shapes, and colors, fostering foundational skills in reading, writing, and math for future success.

Promotes Social Skills

School readiness programs teach children social skills like sharing, cooperation, and taking turns, helping them develop crucial interactions for success in school and overall growth.

Develops Emotional Regulation

School readiness programs help children manage emotions, express feelings appropriately, cope with frustration, and adapt to new routines and environments through guided activities.

Encourages Independence and Self-Care:

Children develop independence and self-care skills, like dressing, managing belongings, and following directions, which are essential for success in the school environment.

Builds Communication Skills

These programs develop verbal and non-verbal communication skills, helping children express ideas, listen, and engage in discussions, which are essential for learning and social interaction.

Fosters Cognitive Development

School readiness activities stimulate cognitive development, enhancing problem-solving, memory, attention, and logical thinking, preparing children for the structured learning environment of school.

Supports Physical Development

Physical activities in school readiness programs promote gross and fine motor skills, essential for tasks like writing, using scissors, and participating in sports and games.

Enhances Confidence and Self-esteem

Acquiring new skills and experiencing success boosts children's confidence and self-esteem, which are crucial for adapting to and succeeding in school.

Prepares for the School Routine

Familiarizing children with school routines, schedules, activity transitions, and classroom rules helps ease their transition to formal education.es.
